You can close this help text by pressing the ?.
You can go back to the parameter menu with the <.
You can close the program by using 'ESC' on your keyboard.


Grid
----
You can move the grid while pressing your right mouse button and moving the mouse.
You can zoom with your mouse wheel.
To place something or remove first click on the symbol then on the grid cells.


Trains
------
This will take you to the train builder where you can place trains and stations.


Reset
-----
This resets the grid to the status it was when this menu opened.


Tracks
------
This allows you to place tracks by clicking a grid cell with you left mouse button.


Eraser
------
This allows you to remove placed tracks by clicking the grid cell with you left mouse button.
