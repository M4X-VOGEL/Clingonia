Grid
----
You can move the grid while pressing your right mouse button and moving the mouse.
You can zoom with your mouse wheel.
To place trains first click on the symbol then on the grid cells.
To place stations see Train List below.
To configure trains see Train List below.
To remove trains see Train List below.



Finish Building
---------------
This will save your environment and the trains and take you to a result screen where you can review it.



Train List
----------
Config:
    - place stations in the same way as trains.
    - configure the earliest departure and latest arrival.
Remove:
    - this will delete the train.



Reset
-----
This resets the grid to the status it was when the track builder opened.



Trains
------
This allows you to place trains by clicking a grid cell with you left mouse button.



Don't Derail Now!
----------------
However, when you've enjoyed the ride,
it's time to pull the brakes.
At any time, you may exit the program
by pressing the ESC key.
