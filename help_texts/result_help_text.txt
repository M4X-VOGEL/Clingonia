Toggle Time Table
-----------------
This will show a table with the train IDs their earliest departure and latest arrival setting and when they actually departed and arrived.
If ActErr appears, it signifies that Clingo's suggested actions failed to guide the train to its intended destination. Consequently, only a spawn move will be executed for that train.



Toggle Gif
----------
This will display an animated gif of the train movements.



Toggle All Paths
-----------------
This will show or hide all train paths



Show/Hide
---------
Here you can show or hide specific train paths
